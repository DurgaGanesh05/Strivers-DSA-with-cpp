Omnichannel Pharmacy Operations Web Platform
Design and develop a responsive, mobile-compatible microservices-based web application for a regional pharmacy and health retail group called Medivault Care, operating across urban stores, hospital-adjacent outlets, and franchise counters. It should include inventory and batch management, prescription and OTC sales with billing, near-real-time stock visibility and replenishment support, BI reporting dashboards, and agentic AI capabilities such as demand recommendations, anomaly detection for expiry or shrinkage, and conversational querying over operational data.
